  FAQ


The project is based on the smart-contract Tron Network, it is fully decentralized and autonomous. The smart contract code is open source, and everyone can study it


To perform transactions to the address of the smart contract and correct work with the website you will need a web extension TronLink or TronPay.


In order to get profit, you need to buy one or several factories for coins


Coins are an in-game Tron Village currency, used to buy factories, profit from which is also added in coins. The exchange rate of coins is fixed: 1 TRX = 25 coins


Any operations concerning the contract can be easily performed in user account on our website.


All profit earned from your factories is equally divided into spare funds and treasury funds. With treasury funds you can buy new buildings for the production development and growth of profits. Spare funds can be withdrawn at any time, they also can be spent on the development of your village. Such a system guarantees flourishing to each village in the game and long-term work of the project in general.


Profit from your factories is added automatically every hour from the moment of the first purchase of coins


The game has a three-level referral system based on the 5% -3% -1% scheme. Each invitee also receives a bonus of 1%. Referral reward is coming only when the first investment is made by the invitees. This will help attract more investors and not load the contract with high commissions for the next investment or reinvestment.


You can save up money in a treasury and buy expensive buildings, or immediately invest earned profit in acquisition of several less pricey buildings. How your village will develop is up to you!


The administration fee is 10% of the coins amount spent for the factories purchase
